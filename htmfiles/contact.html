FAQs

Common Questions

What should I bring to my first class?

Please bring comfortable clothing, a water bottle and a small towel. We provide yoga mats and all necessary equipment.

How early should I arrive?

We recommend arriving 10 - 15 minutes before your first class to complete registration and get oriented with the studio.

What is your cancellation policy?

You can cancel or reschedule your class up to 4 hours before the scheduled time without any penalty.

Do you offer private sessions?

Yes! We offer private sessions for yoga, meditation, fitness training and nutrition counseling. Contact us for pricing and availability.
